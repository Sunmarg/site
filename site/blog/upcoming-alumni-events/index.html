UWC India

A New Phase in the UWC India Movement
The UWC Committee of India and UWC Mahindra College are calling all alumni and supporters into action for taking the UWC movement in the country to the next level. We need greater engagement and involvement from you to help with outreach, promotions, selections and fundraising so as to send the best and the brightest from this vast country into the UWC movement! Please sign up below to be on our mailing list to learn more about opportunities of involvement. 
 
UWC Alumni Social Event: 15 May, Mumbai
On 15th May, Friday in Mumbai, UWC India and UWC Mahindra College are co-hosting an alumni social event to kick off this new phase of engagement. Many of the Heads of the UWC Colleges will be present which makes this an excellent opportunity to catch up with UWC developments worldwide and within your specific college. For UWC India alumni, this will be a meaningful chance to engage with the National Committee which has undergone significant restructuring and will only be a success going forward with broad-based support and engagement from alumni! Please consider attending even if you are based in other parts of India. Your attendance and participation is integral to the success of the new National Committee. 
 
Please sign up here for the UWC India mailing list (for all UWC alumni & friends in India) and RSVP for the Alumni Social.
 
For any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact Maria Charles at [email protected]
 
Advancement Office
UWC Mahindra College

UWC Africa Network Initiative

Waterford Kamhlaba’s representatives will be in Lusaka and Nairobi on May 9, and in Gaborone, Harare, and Dar es Salaam on May 16. All UWC alumni in the area – please join WK alumni for a drink/snack at these cities and meet with WK representatives!
 
In order to know the numbers of people coming and hence better arrange a venue, we urge you to RSVP through filling the following forms below. You should RSVP by May 2 for Lusaka/Nairobi and by May 9 for the other cities.
 
Lusaka :           9 May   Time : 6:00pm
Nairobi             09 May 
Gaborone:        16 May
Harare :           16 May
Dar es Salaam: 16 May

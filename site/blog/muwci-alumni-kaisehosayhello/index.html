MUWCI Alumni: #KaiseHoSayHello

13th Jan 2016 by admin - , (0) Comment

Hey Alumni! Aap kaise ho? As the video says, “we want to keep in touch with you!” and are kicking off 2016 with lots of cool new alumni initiatives, beginning with the first annual #KaiseHoSayHello campaign! Fill out this Update Form to tell us what you’ve been up to and you’ll receive a cheery handwritten postcard from a current student on the hill! If you want more MUWCI in your life here are the ways you can connect with us: 

Alumni Relationship Management

Alumni Hubs

Mentorship Programme

Nominate Peers for Alumni Profiles

Join Your Class Group

Become A Class Agent

Pan-UWC Alumni Platform

 

Snazzy New Alumni Relationship Management System

We’ve moved beyond Excel sheets to relationship management in the cloud! This means that we can now manage your info more systematically, which will make communication so much easier and relevant. We know you’ve done this before, but please fill out your details here one last time (we promise, we will only ask for updates only once a year :)) so that we can send you relevant updates and details about alumni events, mentorship opportunities and networking occasions.

 

Alumni Hubs 

We are all set to launch MUWCI alumni hubs! Some have already been up and going, but now we are fully geared up to rock New York, Boston, London, Delhi, and Bombay. We are looking to organise at least two events in the coming months with all of you. Join your hub today.  

 New York  London  Boston  Delhi  Mumbai

 

Mentor Students

We are excited to begin a mentorship programme that will enable much needed interaction between our current MUWCI lovelies and you. We’ve identified six career focus areas to begin with and are looking for alumni to share their expertise and trace their professional paths into these fields after MUWCI. Using video conferencing we’ll initiate focused panel discussions on each of these areas over the course of this year for interested students and alumni to attend or participate in online. Please write to us at [email protected] to join a panel or nominate a peer doing interesting work in these fields. We will be focusing on the following interest areas in the coming months:

 Environment  STEM  Arts  Social  Entrepreneurship  Development  Health

 

Nominate an Exceptional Peer

You might have noticed that we have been featuring alumni in every newsletter. Help us connect with more alumni doing interesting or unusual things in their paths after MUWCI. Nominate a peer or yourself by writing to us at [email protected]

 

 

Join Your Class Group

Reconnect with your class group on Facebook to share information and news, some laughs and cool adventures with your friends. Join now:

 1999  2000  2001  2002  2003
 2004  2005  2006  2007  2008
 2009  2010  2011  2012  2013
 2014  2015      

 

Class Agents

Become a class agent who helps the MUWCI Advancement Team to connect with the rest of  your class group to communicate major events, reunion updates and opportunities to connect with your friends. Express your interest by writing to us at [email protected]

 

Pan-UWC Alumni Platform

Keep an eye out for the upcoming pan-UWC alumni platform sponsored by UWC International and supported by all UWC schools and colleges. This will be a great place to connect with alumni across UWCs to really expand upon the power of our movement. More details soon. 

 

For any other questions and suggestions, please write to [email protected]

Welcome to our College!

UWC Mahindra College is one of 15 UWC campuses located on five continents. We are an international pre-university residential college located on a 175-acre biodiversity reserve outside of Pune, India. Our unique program combines academic challenge, experiential learning and community living to offer life-defining experiences to young people, enabling them to discover the possibility of change through courageous action, personal example and selfless leadership. Our students are selected from diverse nationalities, socioeconomic statuses and viewpoints based on their potential to take action towards building a more peaceful and sustainable future.

Welcome to UWC Mahindra College!

Working with UWC National Committees, with the other UWC schools and colleges, with thousands of alumni and others committed to the UWC mission, and through our Akshara Programme, we make a difference! We are ambitious, we are idealistic and we are ready to do what needs to be done. We are looking for faculty and students who will gain from and contribute to our community; students who can have a life of impact, students who want to develop their capacity to understand the realities of our world, make sense of them, work with them, students who have the motivation and the potential to tackle injustice, to transform conflict and to confront apathy or indifference concerning the depletion of our natural resources. We seek to empower them with what it needs to make a difference. We "make education a force to unite people, nations and cultures for peace and a sustainable future". If this interests you, get in touch with us or with your UWC National Committee! Our vision is clear and we are on the way: Join us, support us, follow us, inspire us!

Pelham Lindfield Roberts, Head of College

 

Understand Our Core
UWC offers a challenging and transformational education to a deliberately diverse group of young people, inspiring them to become agents of positive change.

CURRICULUM

Our curriculum combines experiential learning, academic challenge and community living for a unique and transformative educational experience, which aims to create agents of change for a more peaceful and sustainable future.

PEOPLE ANDCOMMUNITY



When you ask someone “what makes UWC so special”, the response will often be “the people”. UWC has extraordinary people, who believe that peace and a sustainable future are possible. Our students and faculty come from over 60 countries, representing a diversity that can be found nowhere else in India. The global UWC community is composed of over 60,000 alumni and 130 national committees.

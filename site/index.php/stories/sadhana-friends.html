Sadhana Friends

Engagement, 17th Feb, 2015

The values of experiential learning and service are deeply embedded in the UWC philosophy and the opportunities to work with the local communities challenges our students to rethink and reflect on the idea of social change. Collectively, we strive to reach new heights of development in areas of education and livelihood development.

Students in the Communication & Culture stream of Triveni get to interact and share experiences with Sadhana Friends - a care home for adults with physical or mental disabilities located in the Kolwan Valley. Sadhana Friends is one of the many NGOs partners of UWC Mahindra College that help students learn about, question and participate in the concentric cultural circles of our campus body, the local rural community, and the wider global audience.

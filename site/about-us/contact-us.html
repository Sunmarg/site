Directions

Coming from Mumbai:
Take the Express highway (cross Vashi toll naka, Khallapur toll naka, Talegaon toll naka), cross Wakad Hinjewadi. Come to Chandni Chowk. (take the diversion to Kothrud, take a U turn, come up the road, you reach Chandni Chowk). Take the middle road and follow that road up to 27 kms. You reach Paud Bus stand on the right. Take the right, follow the road for 2 kms, you come to a Y intersection, take the road on the left and follow the road for 5 kms (the road is in a bad condition on this stretch). You reach UWC Mahindra College gate on the right.

Coming from Pune:
Come up to Chandni Chowk. Take the middle road and follow that road up to 27 kms. You reach Paud Bus stand on the right. Take the right, follow the road for 2 kms, you come to a Y intersection, take the road on the left and follow the road for 5 kms (the road is in a bad condition on this stretch). You reach UWC Mahindra College gate on the right.

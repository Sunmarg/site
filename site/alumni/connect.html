Connect

Connect

One of our key initiatives this year is to effectively connect alumni with each other and the current batches. We would like to do this in multiple ways and some of them include alumni hubs, reunions, events and social media.

We are in the process of creating alumni hubs for social gatherings, ideation exercises, and collaboration. Many of our alumni look forward to the 10-year reunions that happen every year and in this section, you can find more information about dates and platforms to connect on. We also want to keep you updated about various events associated with UWC Mahindra College as well as the movement around the world so that you can use the opportunity to connect with alumni globally. Last of all, social media has made communication easier and we want to make the best of it. Be sure to connect with us on various web platforms so that connecting is easy for everyone, and not just those on facebook.

Do take a look at this section to make sure that you are well connected and can find all the information you need related to the college and how you can get involved.

 

The UWC Hub

The UWC Hub is a web platform and mobile app that brings together the UWC community around the world to connect with each other, stay up to date with the many activities happening across the UWC movement, collaborate on projects and causes, discuss and share. The overall aim of the UWC Hub is to strengthen the UWC community, to encourage engagement and positive action, to increase UWC’s collective impact and advance the UWC mission.

The UWC Hub is open to alumni, staff and former staff of all UWC schools and colleges, including UWC Mahindra College.

On 21st September, you will have received a personalised registration link by email to give you access. You can also register directly, but it may take longer to approve you.

Log in to the UWC Hub. 

We look forward to seeing many of you there! For more information, see the FAQ on the UWC website.

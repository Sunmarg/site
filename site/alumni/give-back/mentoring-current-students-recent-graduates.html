Mentoring Current Students & Recent Graduates

Do you miss the UWC experience? One way to feel it again is by chatting with our current students about the university/college(s) you attended and offer suggestions about applying and ways to decide on a specific university/college .You can also mentor students interested in your field/organisation and share knowledge and experiences. Many of our students take a gap year, and look for impactful organisations/groups to dedicate their skills and time to. You could help our recent graduates by creating and offering new gap year options, or explore opportunities for them to benefit from.

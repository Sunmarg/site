Giving Back Financially

Giving Back Financially

We encourage our alumni to give back in anyway they can, and financial contributions are one of the ways you can contribute to the long term economic stability of the college.

Please remember that no amount is too small. You can decide to contribute any amount to specific funds of your choice as listed below

  • Scholarship Fund (100% is used towards student scholarships in the subsequent academic year)
  • Endowment Fund (A long term gift to the college with an untouched principal amount. The interest from this fund goes towards scholarships & annual expenditures in perpetuity)
  • Akshara Foundation (MUWCI’s local impact initiative in the valley focused on education & community development)
  • Gifts towards Specific Projects (carbon neutral capital projects, facilities for music/sports etc, staff accommodation, or another need that I have identified

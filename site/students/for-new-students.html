For New Students

Welcome to UWC Mahindra College

Congratulations on joining the UWC community. We are thrilled to have you here!

This website is designed to give you crucial information and suggestions that will help you settle into life as UWC student. Here, you can find key information about living and studying on the hill, and complete and check the status of forms to ensure your smooth transition to UWC Mahindra College.

This section guides you through some important practical information: forms you need to submit, information about travel and visas, materials you can bring, and FAQs. Alongside, you can discover what project week is all about as well as find out more about the beautiful land of India.

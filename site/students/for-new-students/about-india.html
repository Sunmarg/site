About India

About India

Is this your first time in India? Take a look at some of the suggested readings below to get a sense of the people, culture and the history of this magical land. We picked out readings that provide accurate and factual information about India. Some of them are internet resources and others are books that you may find in the nearest library or a bookstore. We hope that you enjoy reading and get some idea of what this country is about!

General Information

  • http://www.lonelyplanet.com/india
  • http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/285248/India
  • http://forbesindia.com/life/recliner/65-things-we-love-about-india/
  • http://knowindia.gov.in/myindia/myindia_frame.php?id=10

Weather in India

  • http://www.indiaweather.gov.in/

  • Offbeat Tracks in Maharashtra by Milind Gunaji

Suggested Readings on the History of India

Khilnani, Sunil. The Idea of India. England: Hamish Hamilton. 1997. Print.

Wolpert, Stanley. A New History of India. Oxford: Oxford University Press. 1997. Print.

Thapar, Romila. A History of India: Volume 1, New Delhi: Penguin Group. 1966. Print.
